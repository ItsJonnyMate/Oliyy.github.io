About Zendaya Coleman

Zendaya Coleman is considered a triple threat since she has a career in these three things:

  • Acting
  • Dancing
  • Singing

this is a 20 year old woman from Oakland, California who started out as a Disney kid and she still kind of is, but shes is one of the more talented current Disney stars.

Acting

Zendaya started out with Disneys amreican sitcom "Shake it Up" which, personally, was my favorite show and it still is know even though it was taken of the air in 2013 and started in 2010. Shake It Up follows the adventures of best friends CeCe Jones (Bella Thorne) and Rocky Blue (Zendaya) who, with the help of Rocky's brother Ty (Roshon Fegan) and his best friend Deuce Martinez (Adam Irigoyen), fulfill their dreams of becoming professional dancers when they land roles as dancers on a local show, "Shake It Up, Chicago!". But when it ended 2 years later Zendaya got her own tv show called "K.C. Undercover" which i didn't really follow along because i felt like it wasn't the best television show at the moment. Set in Washington, D.C., the series follows K.C. Cooper, a high school math genius who gets recruited by her parents, Craig and Kira Cooper, after she discovers that they are secretly undercover spies working for the Organization. Its actually still going on at the time of this article, but that didn't stop her appearing on Taylor Swift's "Bad Blood", Beyonce's visual album "Lemonade", and ABC's american sitcom "Blackish".

Singing

Zendaya's official music career started in May 30, 2011 coming out with her swaglicious "Swag it Out" single. But it unofficially started in June 11, 2010 when "Watch Me" came out on the album "Shake it Up: Break it Down" for the television show "Shake it Up"

Dancing

Zendaya started dancing when the show "Shake it Up" came out since she starred as a dancer on there. She even continued after it ended because she really enjoyed it. Her favorite kind of dance style is hip-hop since thats the kind of style she performed on "Shake it Up". The first music video she actually had a choreographed routine on was her "Replay".
